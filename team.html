The People Behind Your Care

Your Dental Team in Twickenham

Our team brings together decades of combined experience across all areas of dentistry. We are united by a shared commitment to gentle, honest, patient-first care.

Dr Murtuza Parekh
Principal Dentist

Dr Parekh leads Lebanon Park Dental Group with a commitment to gentle, patient-centred care. Regularly praised by patients for being informative, thorough, and professional, he specialises in general and cosmetic dentistry and oversees all clinical standards at the practice.

Dr Syed G Hassan
Oral Surgeon

Dr Hassan is our specialist Oral Surgeon, handling complex extractions, wisdom tooth removal, and surgical procedures. His expertise means most patients who would otherwise require a hospital referral can be treated on-site at our Twickenham practice.

Dr Wasi Haider
General Dentist

Dr Haider provides comprehensive general dental care including check-ups, restorations, and preventive treatments. He is known for his calm, reassuring approach, particularly with nervous or anxious patients.

Dr Raghu Chhibba
General Dentist

Dr Chhibba brings extensive experience in general and restorative dentistry. He takes time to listen to every patient and provides clear, honest advice to help you make the best decisions for your oral health.

Petya Yordanova
Hygienist

Petya is our experienced dental hygienist, delivering professional cleaning, AirFlow stain removal, and personalised gum health advice. She is passionate about prevention and helping patients achieve long-term oral wellness.

Roohi Awan
Dental Hygienist / Therapist

Roohi is a qualified Dental Hygienist and Therapist, providing both hygiene treatments and a range of preventive dental procedures. She works closely with the clinical team to support patients of all ages.

Aldona Wolska
Dental Nurse

Aldona is a dedicated dental nurse supporting our clinical team to deliver safe, comfortable, and efficient treatments. Her warm manner helps patients feel at ease from the moment they sit in the chair.
