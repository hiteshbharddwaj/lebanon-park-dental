Our Dental Services

From routine check-ups to specialist treatments — comprehensive care for the whole family in Twickenham.

🦷

Dental Hygiene & AirFlow

Professional scale and polish, personalised oral hygiene advice, and the revolutionary Airflow Prophylaxis Master for deep stain and biofilm removal. Regular hygiene visits are the foundation of good oral health and help prevent gum disease.

⭐ 25% off for new patients — Mondays & Thursdays only (min. 45 min appointment)
☀️

Teeth Whitening — Philips Zoom

As an authorised Philips Zoom provider we offer both in-chair whitening (results in under 2 hours) and custom take-home trays for a more gradual approach. Safe, clinically proven, and tailored to your natural tooth shade.

😶

Invisalign Clear Braces

As an official Invisalign Provider, we straighten teeth using a series of custom-made, clear removable aligners. No metal, no wires — most patients achieve their desired result in 6–18 months. We treat both adults and teens.

🧠

Dental Implants

The gold standard for replacing missing teeth. We use 3D digital scanning for precision planning and placement, ensuring natural-looking, long-lasting results. Single implants, implant bridges, and full-arch restorations available.

💊

Cosmetic Dentistry

Porcelain veneers, crowns, white composite fillings, and complete smile makeovers. We use Aidite high-strength ceramic materials for restorations that look and feel completely natural.

🔥

Root Canal Treatment

Save your natural tooth and eliminate infection-related pain. Our experienced team uses modern rotary instruments and careful technique to make the procedure as comfortable and straightforward as possible.

🔨

Oral Surgery

Wisdom tooth removal, surgical extractions, and complex oral surgery procedures carried out on-site by Dr Syed G Hassan, our specialist Oral Surgeon. No need for hospital referrals for most cases.

🚨

Emergency Dental Care

Toothache, cracked tooth, knocked-out tooth, lost crown or filling — we do our best to offer same-day emergency appointments. Call 020 8892 3803 as early as possible.

👤

General & Family Dentistry

Regular check-ups, digital X-rays, gum disease treatment, mouthguards, and preventive advice for patients of all ages. We welcome children from an early age and new patients are always welcome.
